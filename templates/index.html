The economic aspect

Indeed, carpooling will allow you to greatly reduce your costs related to your car journeys (petrol, wear of the car, ...). In the case of alternate carpooling (several drivers who drive alternately) you can divide your travel costs by as many drivers who participate in carpooling. In the case of a carpooling with participation (The passengers participate financially in the journeys), there again we will observe a clear reduction in the costs generated by the use of your car.

A gesture for ecology

Car sharing is a practice that significantly reduces the number of cars on the roads. The first consequence is the reduction in pollution and the emission of greenhouse gases. This also allows the reduction of consumption of non-renewable energy such as petroleum.
